Lines 1 - 2: height and width

Lines 3 - (2 + height): matrix representation of the grid layout
These letter will change throught development
g - grass
d - dirt
w - water
r - rock
s - sand

Line (3 + height): enemy spawn order, letters with no spaces
These letters may change throught development
v - vampire

Line (4 + height): max enemies, the max number of enemies on screen at once

Line (5 + height): enemy spawn cooldown, the number of seconds in between individual enemy spawns

Line (6 + height): enemy spawn pos x, the X grid position of where enemies should spawn, 0 based from left to right

Line (7 + height): enemy spawn pos y, the y grid position of where enemies should spawn, 0 based from top to bottom
